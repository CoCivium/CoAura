# CoPath v0

## Purpose
A CoPath is an open-ended research / inquiry schema, not a closed answer-object.

## Framing
A CoPath should usually be:
- question-framed
- versioned
- indexable
- available for future work
- open to multiple contributors or sessions

## Implications
- prompt templates in CoAcademy should often be framed as CoPaths
- CoPaths should fit naturally into CoIndex
- CoPaths should distinguish:
  - candidate
  - active
  - parked
  - resolved / partially resolved

## Rule
A CoPath remains open unless explicitly closed or superseded.
