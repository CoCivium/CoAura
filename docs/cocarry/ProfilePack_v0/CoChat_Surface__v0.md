# CoChat Surface v0

## Purpose
Turn high-density human rant into:
- structured lanes
- CoTermCandidates
- CoPaths
- optional actions
- safer routing into ops/public surfaces

## Rule
Humans should rant in CoChat-like surfaces, not in ops bloat budgets.

## Benefits
- protects high-potential messy thought
- reduces relay burden
- helps users unload and practice
- supports rationality by reframing without humiliating originators
